Day 1: Identify Your Target Audience\nBefore diving into SEO, it\u2019s crucial to understand who your target audience is. What are their needs, where do they spend their time online, and what search terms might they be using? This information will guide your keyword strategy.<\/p>\n\n<\/div>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t

Day 2-3: Conduct Thorough Keyword Research<\/p>\n\n<\/div>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\n
\n\n

Using tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, or SEMrush, identify the keywords that are relevant to your business and have a good balance of search volume and competition.\n\n<\/p>\n\n<\/div>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\n
\n\n

Based on your keyword research, identify gaps in your content. Plan to create new posts or pages that address these gaps and incorporate the targeted keywords naturally.<\/p>\n\n<\/div>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Day 8-14: On-Page SEO Optimization<\/p>\n\n<\/div>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\n
\n\n

Use tools like Google PageSpeed Insights to analyze and improve the loading speed of your website. This might involve compressing images, leveraging browser caching, or improving server response time.\n
\n
Mobile Optimization\nEnsure your website is mobile-friendly, as mobile usability is a factor in Google\u2019s ranking algorithm. Use the Mobile-Friendly Test tool by Google to check compliance.\n
\n
Fix Broken Links\nUse a tool like Broken Link Checker to find and fix any broken links within your site, which can negatively impact your SEO.\n
\n
XML Sitemap and Robots.txt\nEnsure your XML sitemap is updated and submitted to Google Search Console.
